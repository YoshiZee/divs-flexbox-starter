I was born and raised in Southern California in the city of Cerritos. It was a true melting pot within a melting pot, where I fit right in with my Japanese-Italian heritage.

My passion: I love music! It is the universal language, and has the power to bring people from all walks of life together. There's nothing better than having people sing along with me at a gig!

A fun fact: I've lived in three states during my life: California, Colorado and Arizona. During my time there, I hiked to the top of the highest mountain of each state: Mt. Whitney, Mt. Elbert and Humphreys Peak respectively.
